Bee Gees

1998

The Bee Gees' *One Night Only* album is a live recording of a performance at the MGM Grand in Las Vegas on November 14, 1997. The album was released in 1998. The concert became the group's *One Night Only* world tour, though, the tour was not original intent. Initially, the performance was scheduled to serve as the band’s farewell. Poised in such a pinnacle moment, *One Night Only* captured the Bee Gee’s energy and magic. It included a guest appearance by Céline Dion, a surprise appearance by Barry’s daughter, and a tribute to Barry’s and Maurice’s deceased brother who had been part of the band. For many fans, One Night Only was a utopian moment in their career. It came at an an intended end of a successful 30-year career. The performance and album included 32 songs, and the album was an instant favorite world-wide, inciting platinum sales and hitting number 1 on charts.
